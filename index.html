The Three Pillars
01
Risk Cadence Engine
AI continuously monitors schedule, cost, safety, and quality signals across all active work packages. Weekly risk registers auto-generate with severity scoring, trend lines, and recommended mitigations — so your team stops reacting and starts anticipating. No more Friday-afternoon fire drills.
02
Sequencing Logic
The OS understands predecessor dependencies, long-lead procurement, crew constraints, and site access windows. Dynamic schedule logic flags sequencing conflicts before they cascade, runs "what-if" scenarios on demand, and keeps critical-path clarity visible to every stakeholder at all times.
03
Reporting & Automation Stack
Owner dashboards, lender reports, GC look-aheads, and executive summaries — all generated automatically from a single data model. Eliminate manual slide-deck assembly. Close the month in hours, not days. Version-controlled, audit-ready, and formatted for each audience.
